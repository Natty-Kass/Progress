About Progress Academy

Welcome to Progress Academy, a distinguished educational institution located in the heart of Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. Since our establishment in 1998 G.C., we have been dedicated to providing quality education that empowers students for success and shapes them into well-rounded individuals.

At Progress Academy, we offer a comprehensive educational journey from kindergarten to grade 12, following Ethiopia's national curriculum. Our mission is to foster a nurturing and stimulating environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. We believe in producing not just good students, but future leaders equipped with the knowledge and skills to excel in a rapidly changing world.

Our campus is equipped with modern facilities, including state-of-the-art science labs, computer labs, and a spacious sports field. These resources support both our rigorous academic programs and a wide range of extracurricular activities such as sports and arts. We believe in the importance of a balanced education that promotes creativity, teamwork, and personal growth.

Many times Progress Academy was honored with a gold prize for the quality of our education, standing out among all private schools in Addis Ababa. This achievement reflects our commitment to excellence and continuous improvement in everything we do.

As a unique feature, Progress Academy also embraces the rich cultural heritage of Russia, integrating aspects of Russian education into our programs. This distinctive approach further enriches our students' learning experience, broadening their perspectives and enhancing their global awareness.
